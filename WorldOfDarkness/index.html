World of Darkness

The World of Darkness has a splat book, or a book that expands the games options, that is very celtic-like. The book is called Changeling.

Changelings

In the story of the changelings, you play someone who was kidnapped by the Fae; but unlike most you found your way back home. You get some powers on the way, and different things can happen depending on the story the GM creates.

Besides this feeling of Celtic-ness we don't see many other things that really show celtic inspiration with the setting. There could be small theme changes that a GM could do to make sure that the game sticks closer to the feeling as well, especially if they wiser about Celtic concepts.
